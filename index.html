// What It Is

One Tower.
Four Resources.

The Azzopardi Tower is a single integrated structure that simultaneously generates electricity, extracts clean drinking water from the atmosphere, purifies the surrounding air, and produces hydrogen fuel — powered entirely by the Sun, with nothing wasted and nothing emitted.

It combines two original systems designed by M.J. Azzopardi — the TriCore V2 Solar Energy Matrix and the ART-01 Atmospheric Resource Tower — into one unified structure where each system powers and feeds the other in a closed loop.

The TriCore generates more power than it needs. That surplus powers the ART-01 atmospheric water system. The ART-01 produces more water than it needs. That surplus feeds the hydrogen electrolyser. The hydrogen stores energy for the night. At night that energy powers everything. One tower. One loop. No waste.

// How It Works

From Sunlight
to Everything

🌞
TriCore V2

Thousands of heliostat mirrors surround the tower base, tracking the Sun and focusing all that light onto the tower tip. At the tip a ceramic core heats to 1,400°C. That heat converts directly to electricity across a vacuum gap 85 nanometres wide — no turbines, no moving parts.

Waste heat from the process feeds the water and air systems below. Surplus electricity goes to hydrogen production. Molten salt stores thermal energy for overnight operation.

OUTPUT — 5–20 MW CONTINUOUS · 24/7 WITH THERMAL STORAGE
💧
ART-01

Air is drawn in from the upper tower. It passes over cooling coils — the temperature drop causes moisture to condense and collect. That water passes through three filtration stages: sediment, activated carbon, and UV sterilisation. Clean drinking water flows to storage below.

The cooling system that drives this process runs entirely on TriCore surplus power. The waste heat from the TriCore boosts condensation efficiency. Two systems, one loop.

OUTPUT — UP TO 300,000 LITRES PER DAY AT FULL SCALE
🌿
Air Purification

Air drawn through the tower passes through carbon capture filters and electrostatic particulate removal before being released back into the atmosphere at mid-tower level. The air that leaves is cleaner than the air that entered.

In urban deployments this creates a continuous purification effect on the surrounding environment — the tower breathes the city's air and returns it clean.

OUTPUT — CONTINUOUS · CARBON CAPTURE + PARTICULATE REMOVAL
🔋
Hydrogen Fuel

Surplus electricity from the TriCore routes to an electrolyser that splits water into hydrogen and oxygen. The hydrogen is compressed and stored in tanks at the tower base — available as long-term energy reserve, grid export, or vehicle fuel.

Overnight, stored hydrogen and molten salt thermal energy keep the entire tower running without sunlight. The system never stops.

OUTPUT — GENERATED DURING SURPLUS · DAYS TO WEEKS RESERVE
// The Integrated Loop

Why Integration
Changes Everything

Standalone, both systems have weaknesses. The TriCore needs water for its cooling and electrolysis loops. The ART-01 needs power to run its fans and condensation system. Integrated, each system's surplus becomes the other system's fuel.
